Best Cars and Bikes in the World!!!

Ford Mustang GT

This is a classic American muscle car. It has a V8 engine and makes a very loud noise when you drive it fast.

Ducati Panigale

This is a super fast red bike from Italy. It looks very cool but it is dangerous if you don't know how to ride.

Tesla Cybertruck

It looks like a metal triangle. It is electric and bulletproof. Some people love it and some people hate it.

Kawasaki Ninja

The green monster. This bike is very popular for racing and it goes extremely fast on the highway.

Toyota Supra

Legendary JDM car. Tuners love this car because you can modify the engine to have 1000 horsepower.

Harley Davidson

For people who like to cruise slowly and wear leather jackets. It has a very distinct sound.
